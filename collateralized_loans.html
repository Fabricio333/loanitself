Collateralized Loans Explained

Understand how borrowing against your bitcoin works.

How Collateral Works

You lock up bitcoin as collateral. The lender holds it while you receive dollars. If the loan isn't repaid, the collateral can be sold to cover the debt.

What Happens on a Price Drop

If bitcoin's value falls too much, you may need to add collateral or repay part of the loan to avoid liquidation.
