The Loan That Pays Itself

Smart Bitcoin-backed strategies for keeping your sats while spending.

BTC went up! Alice buys back her debt with fewer sats.

Strategy 1: Spend USD Today, Repay Fewer Sats Tomorrow

🪙 Alice posts 1 BTC as collateral ($50K)
💵 Takes a $25K loan
🚀 BTC rises to $100K
↩️ Repays loan with 0.25 BTC instead of 0.5

\( \text{Repay in BTC} = \frac{\$25,000}{\text{BTC Price at Repayment}} = 0.25\,\text{BTC} \)
As BTC grows, Alice keeps borrowing more without selling.

Strategy 2: Releverage on the Way Up

🪙 Alice starts with 2 BTC ($100K)
💸 Takes $40K loan (40% LTV)
📈 BTC doubles → new value $200K
🔁 Takes second $40K loan

\( \text{New LTV} = \frac{40K}{200K} = 20\% \Rightarrow \text{Room to Releverage} \)
She spent, she stacked, and the stack paid back the debt.

Strategy 3: Spend + Stack + Self-Repay

🪙 2 BTC collateral = $100K
💵 Borrows $50K → $30K shopping 🛍️, $20K buys 0.4 BTC
🚀 BTC doubles → 0.4 BTC = $40K
↩️ Loan can be repaid with gains

\( \text{BTC Gain} = 0.4 \times 100K = \$40,000 \)
